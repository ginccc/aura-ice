The Art of the Perfect Chill

We've reinvented ice to be the most exciting part of your drink.

Crystal-Clear Perfection

Our proprietary, 17-stage purification and freezing process removes all impurities and micro-bubbles, resulting in a flawless, glass-like sphere.

Slow-Melting by Design

The dense structure and large surface area mean our spheres melt up to 5x slower than regular cubes, chilling your drink perfectly without dilution.

Botanical Infusion

Elevate your drink with infusions of natural botanicals. As the ice melts, it releases aromatic notes of citrus, herbs, or spices for a dynamic experience.

An Effortless Experience

From our door to your glass in three simple steps.

1. Choose Your Aura

Select from our curated infusions, like zesty grapefruit-rosemary, or opt for pure, unflavored perfection.

2. Unbox & Pour

Our sustainably-packaged ice is delivered to your door. Simply place a sphere in your glass and pour your drink over it.

3. Experience the Shift

Watch as the ice chills without diluting, slowly releasing its aromatic essence and transforming your beverage sip by sip.
